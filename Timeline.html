Timeline Of INTERNET

Here is a brief timeline of the history of Internet.
1960s: The idea of a decentralized network that could connect computers is developed.
1969: The first message is sent between two computers on ARPANET, a precursor to the Internet
1970s: Email is developed, and the first computer virus is created. 1980s: The first domain name is registered, and the World Wide Web is invented.
1990s: The first web browser is released, and the first online search engine, Archie, is developed.
2000s: Social networking sites such as MySpace and Facebook become popular, and e-commerce sites like Amazon and eBay thrive
2010s: The rise of smartphones and mobile devices leads to the development of mobile apps and mobile-friendly websites. Cloud computing becomes widespread, and online streaming services like Netflix and YouTube grow in popularity.
Present day: The Internet continues to evolve, with the development of new technologies like virtual reality and the Internet of Things, as well as growing concerns over cybersecurity and online privacy.
